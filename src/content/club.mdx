# Official GoGo Groundhogs Club

<br />

The Official GoGo Groundhogs Club is a special Discord server reserved only for GoGo Groundhog holders. The Official GoGo Groundhogs Club will feature access to early minting when the GoGo Groundhogs Collection launches, special giveaways for GoGo Groundhog holders only, and exclusive access to mini GoGo Groundhog collections (such as possible 3D collections or special rarities.) To unlock all the perks offered in the Official GoGo Groundhogs Club, you must mint or win at least 1 GoGo Groundhog NFT.
