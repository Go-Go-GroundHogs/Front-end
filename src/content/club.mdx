# Alpha Groundhog Club

<br />

The Official Alpha Groundhog Club is a special Discord channel (text and voice) reserved only for Genesis GoGo Groundhog Collection holders. The Official Alpha Groundhog Club will feature access to early minting when the Genesis GoGo Groundhogs Collection launches, special giveaways for Genesis GoGo Groundhog holders only, the ability to vote on future project decisions as a Genesis GoGo Groundhog holder in the form of a DAO, and exclusive access to mini GoGo Groundhog collections (such as possible 3D collections or special rarities.)

To unlock all the perks offered in the Official Alpha Groundhog Club, you must mint or win at least 1 Genesis GoGo Groundhog Collection NFT and maintain the asset/s to remain in the Club. Should you sell your Genesis GoGo Groundhog assets, you will be removed from the Club channels and will no longer have access to the numerous perks and benefits you can find in the Alpha Groundhog Club.
